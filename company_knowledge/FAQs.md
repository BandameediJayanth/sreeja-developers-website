## General

### Is Sreeja Highway City RERA approved?
Yes. Registration number: **TSRERA/P02400039281**. Verify on the official TSRERA portal.

### What approvals does the layout have?
HMDA and DTCP approved layouts meeting government planning standards.

### Can I get a bank loan for an open plot?
Yes — major banks offer plot loans up to 60–70% with clean titles and HMDA approvals, subject to credit profile.

### How do I verify the legal title?
Request the Legal Vault pack: 30-year link documents, title search reports, and layout approvals.

### How can NRIs register without traveling to India?
Via registered Power of Attorney (POA) to a family member in India. Legal team assists with embassy procedures.
