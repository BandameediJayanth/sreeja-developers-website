## Key Takeaways for Sreeja

### Adopt (Luxury — Dubai/India Premium)
- Full-bleed drone video hero
- Dark charcoal + gold palette
- VIP registration CTAs
- Minimal clutter, dramatic typography

### Adopt (Utility — USA/Singapore)
- Interactive SVG plot maps
- Financial calculators (EMI, stamp duty)
- Programmatic SEO corridor pages
- JSON-LD structured data

### Avoid
- Mobile pop-up overload (Indian portal pattern)
- Hidden legal documents
- Static PDF master plans without interactivity
- Generic cheap pricing language
