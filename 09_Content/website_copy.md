### PAGE 7: FAQs (`/faqs`)

#### 2.1 SEO Metadata
*   **Meta Title:** Frequently Asked Questions | Sreeja Developers
*   **Meta Description:** Find answers to common questions about Sreeja Highway City plots, legal status, bank loans, registration process, and NRI buying guides.

#### 2.2 Frequently Asked Questions Content

##### Q1: Is Sreeja Highway City RERA approved?
*   **Answer:** Yes, absolutely. Sreeja Highway City is registered under the Telangana Real Estate Regulatory Authority. The official registration number is **TSRERA/P02400039281**. You can verify this on the official TSRERA portal.

##### Q2: What approvals does the layout have?
*   **Answer:** The layout has approved permits from the Hyderabad Metropolitan Development Authority (HMDA) and the Directorate of Town and Country Planning (DTCP). This guarantees that the layout meets government planning standards.

##### Q3: Can I get a bank loan for purchasing an open plot?
*   **Answer:** Yes. Because of our clean legal titles and HMDA approvals, major public and private banks (including SBI, HDFC, and ICICI) provide plot purchase loans up to 60-70% of the property value, subject to your credit profile.

##### Q4: How do I verify the legal title of the land?
*   **Answer:** Sreeja maintains a transparent "Legal Vault." Upon registering your interest, we will provide a full pack of 30-year link documents, title search reports vetted by senior lawyers, and layout approvals for your independent legal review.

##### Q5: How can NRIs register a plot without traveling to India?
*   **Answer:** NRIs can easily register their plots through a registered Power of Attorney (POA) designated to a family member in India. Our legal team will draft the POA document and guide you through the process at your local Indian Embassy.
